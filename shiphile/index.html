What do you need to prepare?
  • To make an appointment

    Our club will hold skydiving every Friday, you just need to email or call us one day in advance to make an appointment for a medical examination.

    View Details
  • The deposit

    After passing the medical examination, you need to pay a deposit of $100 per person, and the subsequent fees will be settled after the skydive is completed.

    View Details
  • Ready to skydive

    The plane will climb to the desired altitude in 15 to 20 minutes, and the whole process from jumping out to landing will take about 7 to 8 minutes in the air.
